Train Mystery

Controls: WASD or Arrow Keys to move

Interaction: Press E when near an NPC to talk

Dialogue: Type your message and press Enter

Dialogue Scroll: Up/Down arrows to scroll long text

Exit Dialogue: Press Escape

Clues Display: Press C to show/hide collected clues

Level Complete: Press Enter to go to next level

Game Complete: Press R to restart from level 1

Objective: Collect clues by talking to NPCs in order!
